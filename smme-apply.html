Eligibility

Basic Requirements

To qualify for SMME funding through NFS Insure, your business should meet the following basic criteria.

Registered Business

Your business must be registered with CIPC (Pty Ltd, CC, Sole Prop or Partnership).

Minimum 6 Months Trading

Your business should have been actively trading for at least 6 months with verifiable turnover.

Monthly Revenue

Minimum monthly turnover of R30 000 or more, evidenced by 3–6 months bank statements.

SA ID / Valid Passport

The business owner or director must hold a valid South African ID or passport.

Business Bank Account

A dedicated business bank account is required. Personal accounts are not accepted.

South Africa Based

Your business must be registered and actively operating within the Republic of South Africa.

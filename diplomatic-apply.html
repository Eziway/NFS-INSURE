Before You Apply

Application Requirements

Your mission must meet the following criteria to be eligible for the Concierge Diplomatic Desk programme.

Recognised Diplomatic Entity

Must be a registered Embassy, High Commission, Consulate, or accredited International Organization.

Official Accreditation

Proof of diplomatic accreditation recognized by the South African Department of International Relations (DIRCO).

Authorized Representative

Application must be submitted by an Ambassador, Deputy Chief of Mission, or designated authorized officer.

Operational in South Africa

Your mission must be currently operational, or in the process of establishing operations within South Africa.

Genuine Service Need

The mission must have a current or upcoming requirement for at least one insurance or financial service.

Documents Ready to Submit

All required supporting documents must be available and uploaded at the time of application.

Should you not be contacted within 3 business days of applying, please contact us at info@nfs.insure.
